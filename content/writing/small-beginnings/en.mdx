This place is meant to be quiet. No push notifications, no engagement metrics, no algorithmic feed telling you what someone else thinks you'll like next.

Just a corner of the internet where I can leave a few notes for myself and, if you happen to wander in, for you too.

I don't know how often I'll write here. Probably not much. That feels right. The best letters I ever received arrived months apart, from people who took their time saying whatever they wanted to say.

If you're here, welcome.
